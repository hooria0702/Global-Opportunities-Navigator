About OppsNavigator

Why this was built and how it works.

The Problem

Pakistani students face a real challenge when searching for international scholarships and exchange programmes. Information is scattered across dozens of websites, eligibility criteria are buried in PDFs, and deadlines are easy to miss. Many students apply to programmes they don't qualify for — or worse, never apply to ones they do qualify for because they didn't know they existed.

What This Does

OppsNavigator centralises opportunities and runs a transparent eligibility check against your academic profile. Enter your CGPA, degree level, and language scores — the system checks every programme's requirements and tells you exactly where you stand.

  • Checks 8 criteria per programme — GPA, degree level, IELTS, TOEFL, field, country, funding, deadline
  • Shows a 0–100 match score with a breakdown of each factor
  • Flags exactly what's missing and why
  • Tracks upcoming deadlines for programmes you're eligible for
  • Lets you save a shortlist for later

How the Eligibility Check Works

No machine learning. The logic is fully rule-based and explainable:

Hard failures → Not Eligible
Wrong degree level, missing required IELTS/TOEFL, GPA more than 0.2 below minimum, deadline passed.
Soft gaps → Potentially Eligible
Field mismatch, country not in preferred list, funding type mismatch, GPA within 0.2 of minimum.
